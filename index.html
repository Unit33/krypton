About Krypton Capital

A leading early-stage venture firm focused on media, finance, and cutting-edge technologies.

Krypton Capital’s investment experience ranges across a variety of sectors, including Media, Technology, Financial Services, Healthcare, Blockchain and more.

In 2017, Krypton Capital was founded by Ilan Tzorya to invest in technology, media and crypto-centric businesses worldwide and to establish a strong eco-system of complimenting ventures which help each other succeed.

Our goal is to ultimately turn great ideas into even better companies, increase the value and revenues of our partners and benefit investors. In pursuance of this, we have partnered up with a host of strong teams and passionate entrepreneurs from various industries and have helped companies raise over $300M.

Years of team expirience

We are very optimistic about the future of blockchain and other emerging technologies, and have chosen to focus on the following categories:

  1. Software solutions for various industries, including blockchain
  2. Online marketing (including media, marketing agencies, and affiliate networks)
  3. Online news and data
  4. Global payment solutions
  5. Token sales and crowdfunding companies
  6. Education & healthcare
